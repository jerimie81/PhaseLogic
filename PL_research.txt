# PhaseLogic: Marketing Research & Strategy Plan
Target: Professional AI Software Engineering for Everyone

## 1. Executive Summary
The central thesis of PhaseLogic is the democratization of professional software engineering. By moving beyond simple chat-based code generation to a structured, multi-agent pipeline, PhaseLogic allows anyone to create production-ready software. This plan researches how to market the technical advantage of "Capability-Based Orchestration" — using the right LLM for the right task (e.g., Gemini for build tools, Claude for UI, etc.) — as the gold standard for AI-driven development.

## 2. Market Analysis Research
*   **Segment A: The "Non-Technical Founder"**: Research the pain points of individuals with ideas but no coding skills. How much do they currently spend on MVP development?
*   **Segment B: The "Efficiency-Seeking Pro"**: Research how professional developers can use PhaseLogic to handle "scaffolding" and "plumbing," focusing their human effort on high-level logic.
*   **The "LLM Fatigue" Factor**: Research user sentiment regarding the inconsistency of single-model code generation. Position PhaseLogic as the solution to "model hallucination" through cross-model validation.

## 3. Core Marketing Pillars (The "Why")
*   **Multi-Agent Superiority**: Research and document case studies where a multi-model approach (PhaseLogic) outperformed a single-model approach (standard GPT-4/Claude chat).
*   **Quality Gates as a Product**: Market the fact that PhaseLogic *refuses* to publish bad code. This "AI Architect" persona is a unique selling point compared to "AI autocomplete."
*   **Zero-Config Professionalism**: Highlight the "Build Report" as the artifact that proves the software is investor-ready.

## 4. Technical Deep-Dive Research for Content Marketing
*   **The Orchestration Advantage**: Develop a white paper on the "Capability Matching Score" implemented in v1. Explain why a profile-based approach is more resilient than hard-coding model names.
*   **The Phase Logic**: Explain the 6-phase pipeline as a mirror of professional Agile/SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle) practices.
*   **Security & Ethics**: Research and promote the "Security Audit" and "Quality Gate" features as the ethical way to build with AI.

## 5. Competitive Landscape
*   **Vs. Cursor/Windsurf**: Research how we differ (Workflow Orchestration vs. IDE enhancement).
*   **Vs. Devin/OpenDevin**: Research our accessibility. PhaseLogic is a structured tool for outcomes, not just a "simulated developer."
*   **Vs. No-Code (Bubble/FlutterFlow)**: Research the advantage of owning the actual source code vs. being locked into a proprietary platform.

## 6. Campaign Strategy
*   **The "Prompt-to-Product" Challenge**: Create a series of 5-minute videos showing a project going from a single-sentence description to a published GitHub PR with a Build Report.
*   **The "Agent Marketplace"**: Tease the upcoming Agent Configurator as a way for users to "hire" specialized AI talent.
*   **Template Demos**: Use the "Reproducible Demo Templates" (Enhancement 9) as lead magnets.

## 7. Success Metrics
*   **Conversion**: From "Intake Description" to "Successful Phase 6 Run."
*   **Retention**: Projects resumed vs. projects abandoned.
*   **Quality**: Percentage of projects that pass the Quality Gate on the first attempt.
